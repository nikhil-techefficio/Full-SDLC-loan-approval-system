# Stakeholder Analysis

## Primary Stakeholders
- Borrower / Applicant
- Loan Officers
- Bank Operations Team

## Secondary Stakeholders
- Risk Management Team
- Compliance & Audit Team
- IT & Security Team

## Stakeholder Needs
Borrower:
- Transparent status tracking
- Secure document upload
- Clear reasons for approval/rejection

Loan Officer:
- Manual override capability
- Justification capture
- Prioritized review queue

Compliance & Audit:
- Explainable decisions
- Immutable audit trail
- Regulatory reporting

IT & Security:
- Secure data handling
- High availability
- Controlled access



# Loan Approval Workflow

1. Applicant submits loan application
2. System validates input data
3. Credit bureau check is triggered
4. Rule engine evaluates eligibility
5. Decision outcomes:
   - Auto-Approve
   - Auto-Reject
   - Manual Review
6. KYC documents verified post-approval
7. Final decision recorded with audit trail

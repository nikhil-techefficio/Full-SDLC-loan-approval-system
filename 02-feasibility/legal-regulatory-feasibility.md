# Legal & Regulatory Feasibility

## Applicable Regulations
- RBI data handling guidelines
- IT Act, 2000
- Data privacy requirements

## Compliance Measures
- No black-box AI in decisioning
- Full audit trail for decisions
- Data masking for sensitive identifiers

## Data Residency
- Data stored within approved regions

## Explainability
- Rule-based decisions logged explicitly

## Verdict
Legally feasible if compliance controls are enforced.

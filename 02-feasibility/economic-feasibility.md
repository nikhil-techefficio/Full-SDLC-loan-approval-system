# Economic Feasibility

## Development Cost (Rough Order)
- Backend Dev: 2 months
- Frontend Dev: 1 month
- QA & Hardening: 1 month

## Infrastructure Cost (Monthly Estimate)
- Compute (Cloud / VPS): ₹15,000
- Database: ₹8,000
- Storage (Docs): ₹5,000
- Monitoring & Logs: ₹3,000

Estimated Total: ₹30,000–₹35,000 / month

## Cost Optimizations
- Start on free-tier / local Docker
- Scale infra only after load increases

## Business Value
- Approval time reduced from days → seconds
- Reduced operational staff load
- Improved compliance traceability

## Verdict
Economically viable for MVP and early scale.
